Effective Date: 29 March, 2024

Be respectful

Users must maintain a respectful and courteous demeanor towards each other at all times. Any form of harassment, discrimination, or offensive behavior is strictly prohibited.

Fair Play

Users are expected to abide by the rules and regulations set forth by the bot to ensure fair and equitable gameplay for all participants. Cheating, exploiting loopholes, or engaging in any unfair practices are not tolerated.

Privacy

Users should respect the privacy and confidentiality of others. Avoid sharing personal information without consent, and refrain from infringing upon others' privacy rights.

Time waste

Respect the value of time for yourself and others. Avoid unnecessary delays and prolonged inactivity during matches or interactions. Promptly respond to messages and requests to close the gameplay experiences.

Bug report

If you encounter any bugs or technical issues while using BrawlBattle, promptly report it. Provide detailed information about the bug, including when and how it occurred, to facilitate quick resolution. Avoid exploiting or abusing bugs for personal gain, as it undermines the integrity of the game and fairness for all players. Your cooperation in identifying and resolving bugs contributes to improving the overall gaming experience for everyone.

No spam

Maintain respectful and relevant communication within the private chat provided by the bot, refraining from spamming, advertising, or using inappropriate language or content.

Age Restrictions

Users must meet the minimum age requirement specified by the discord TOS.

Disclamer

Take note of the following disclaimer: The sign-up feature provided by this bot is for entertainment and fun purposes only. While we strive to provide accurate and enjoyable matches, we do not guarantee their suitability or accuracy. By using this bot, you acknowledge and accept this disclaimer.

Punishments

If you ignore any of these rules may result in disciplinary action, including mute, kick or permanent ban from the Discord server, or bot's features.

For any questions, concerns or issue, please contact us.

Thank you for using BrawlBattle!
